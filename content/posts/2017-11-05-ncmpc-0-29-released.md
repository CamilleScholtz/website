ncmpc
[0.29](/download/ncmpc/0/ncmpc-0.29.tar.xz) has
been released.

It fixes two crash bugs after connection failure, re-adds the
"lyrics_plugin_dir" compile-time option and allows detecting
libncurses without pkg-config.
