**Music Player Daemon**
[0.20.14](/download/mpd/0.20/mpd-0.20.14.tar.xz)
has been released.

It fixes state file corruption in the presence of mount points and a
Solaris build failure.  Locking in all archive plugins has been
optimized to reduce xruns.
