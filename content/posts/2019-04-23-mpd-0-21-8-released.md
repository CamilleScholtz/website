**Music Player Daemon**
[0.21.8](http://www.musicpd.org/download/mpd/0.21/mpd-0.21.8.tar.xz)
has been released.

A SoundCloud regression has been fixed.  The `smbclient` plugin
enables buffering.  Various build failures have been fixed.
